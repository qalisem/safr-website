Data We Collect

Location data ‚Äî Safr uses your device's GPS to provide navigation, calculate prayer times for your current position, determine Qibla direction, and find nearby mosques. Location data is processed on your device and sent to mapping services (Mapbox, Google Places) only as needed for routing and place search. We do not store your location history on our servers.

Locally stored preferences ‚Äî Your settings (prayer calculation method, madhab, corridor width, saved locations like Home/Work, recent destinations) are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.

No accounts or personal information ‚Äî Safr does not require an account. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any other personal identifiers.

What Safr Is

Safr is a navigation app that integrates Islamic prayer times and mosque locations into route planning. It is designed to help Muslim travelers plan prayer stops during road trips.

What Safr Is NOT

Safr is not a replacement for your own judgment while driving. Prayer times are calculated algorithmically and may differ slightly from your local mosque's announced times. Mosque information is sourced from public databases and may not always be current (hours, open/closed status, etc.).

Safe Driving

Do not interact with the app while driving. Set up your route before you start driving. Voice guidance and prayer alerts are designed to minimize the need to look at your screen. You are solely responsible for safe operation of your vehicle.

Accuracy

We strive for accuracy in prayer times, mosque locations, and routing, but we cannot guarantee perfection. Prayer times are calculated using established North American astronomical methods (ISNA, MWL) and may vary by 1-2 minutes from other sources. Mosque data comes from OpenStreetMap and Google Places and may contain inaccuracies.

Safr is currently available in the United States and Canada. Mosque coverage and routing quality may vary by region. The app requires an internet connection for navigation and mosque search.
